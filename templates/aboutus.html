About SRVR

Who We Are

Welcome to SRVR, where passion for food meets the convenience of doorstep delivery. We are more than just a food delivery service; we are culinary enthusiasts dedicated to bringing you a delightful and nutritious dining experience without leaving the comfort of your home.

Our Values

Here at SRVR: our mission is simple: to make every meal memorable. We believe that taste and time doesn't have to be sacrificed when making nutritious and cost-effective meals. We value everyone's schedules and dietary preferences to fit each lifestyle. That's why we're committed to curating a diverse selection of top-notch cuisines, ensuring there's something for every taste bud and occasion.

Our Story

In 2023, we realized that the food delivery business has become more and more expensive over the years, and we have discovered that inflation has prevented a lot of lower income residents from having access to nutritious, delicious, and time saving meals. With SRVR we aim to offer healthy and simple meal options that fit any budget and lifestlye.
