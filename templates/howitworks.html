How SRVR Works

  1. Browse the Menu

    Explore a diverse menu offering a variety of delectable dishes. Navigate through different categories and dietary restrictions ranging from global dishes to vegan comfort meals.

  2. Select Meals

    Choose your favorite meals from the menu. Utilize the meal selection navbar to filter meals based on your preferences. Click each meal to get a glimpse of the recipe and more details.

  3. Add to Cart

    Found a meal you love? Click "Add to Cart" to include it in your order

  4. View Cart

    Curious about what's in your cart? Navigate to the Cart section to review your selected meals. Ensure everything looks perfect before proceeding.

  5. Checkout

    Ready to checkout? Head to the checkout for a seamless ordering process. If you haven't logged in, you'll be prompted to do so or create an account for a personalized experience.

  6. Order Confirmation

    Once you've completed the checkout process, you'll receive an order confirmation. Sit back, relax, and await the culinary delights to arrive at your doorstep.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of meals do we offer?
We offer a diverse range of meals to suit various preferences and dietary requirements. Our menu includes global cuisines including Asian, Italian, and Mexican dishes. We also have a variety of options for different dietary preferences, such as vegetarian and vegan meals.
Is a subscription required?
Yes, we offer a subscription-based service to make meal planning even more convenient for our customers. You can choose a plan that suits your needs, and you can easily skip or cancel anytime.
Can I customize dietary preferences?
We offer a wide range of meal plans for your specific dietary preferences and allergies.
Where do we deliver?
For now, we only ship within the US.
How is food packaged?
We package our ingredients to ensure freshness. The meal kits include pre portioned ingredients, using insulated packaging with ice packs to keep everything fresh.
What if I am not home for delivery?
Our packages will keep your ingredients safe even if you are not home when the package arrives.
How can I contact Customer Support?
You can contact customer support team through support@srvr.com
Which payment methods do we accept?
We currently accept Disover, Mastercard, and Visa.
How can I cancel or modify my subscription?
You can easily manage your subscription through your account on our website.
